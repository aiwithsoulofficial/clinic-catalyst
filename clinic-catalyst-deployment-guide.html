Client Onboarding Playbook

End-to-end deployment process from signed contract to live system
What We Need From The Client (Onboarding Call)
□ Business name, address, phone, email, website URL
□ Logo (PNG, high res) + any brand colours/fonts they use
□ Service menu with pricing (treatments, durations, costs)
□ Team member names, roles, and availability (who does what, when)
□ Current booking system (Cliniko, Phorest, Timely, Fresha, etc.)
□ Facebook page admin access (for ads)
□ Instagram account access (for DM automation)
□ Google Business Profile access (for GEO - Package 2+)
□ Any current ad accounts / Meta Business Suite access
□ Key offer or promotion they want to lead with (e.g. "$199 Venus Viva")
□ Any compliance notes (TGA restrictions, Schedule 4 products they use)
□ Preferred greeting style / tone (clinical vs warm vs luxury)

01
Onboarding & Access
Day 1-2
✓
Onboarding call - collect all access and info
Walk through the checklist above. Get all logins, brand assets, service menus, team details. Screen share and collect what we can live on the call. Record the call.
Us
✓
Create GHL sub-account for the clinic
Set up their CRM workspace. Import contacts if they have an existing list. Configure business details, timezone, and branding.
Us
✓
Client sends remaining assets within 48 hours
Anything we couldn't collect on the call - logo files, team headshots, detailed pricing sheets, Facebook admin invites.
Client
02
Aria Voice Agent Build
Day 2-4
✓
Build clinic knowledge base
Create the knowledge document from their service menu, pricing, team bios, FAQs, opening hours, policies. This is what Aria knows about the clinic.
Us
✓
Deploy Aria voice agent
Clone from working template. Swap clinic name, services, team, greeting, offer. Configure voice (Becca default, Australian). Connect to GHL calendar for real-time availability checks and booking.
Us
✓
Configure GHL calendar and booking
Set up appointment types matching their services. Map practitioner availability. Connect booking confirmation emails and SMS reminders.
Us
✓
Connect phone number
Assign dedicated phone number to Aria. Configure inbound and outbound calling. Test call flow end-to-end: ring > Aria answers > qualifies > checks availability > books.
Us
✓
TGA compliance check
Verify Aria never names Schedule 4 products out loud. Test edge cases - "do you do Botox?" should redirect to consultation without naming the product. Review all prompts.
Us
Critical: Test 5+ inbound calls and 3+ outbound calls before going live. Verify Aria books into the correct calendar, sends confirmation, and handles "I need to check with my partner" gracefully. Never go live without end-to-end testing.
03
Ads & Lead Generation
Day 3-5
✓
Set up Meta Ads account
Connect Facebook page, configure pixel, create custom audiences. Set up lead form or landing page for the hero offer. Alicia handles creative and targeting strategy.
Us
✓
Build ad creatives
3-5 ad variations (image + video) featuring the hero offer. Use clinic's own imagery where possible. Copy focused on the outcome, not the treatment name.
Us
✓
Configure lead-to-call workflow
When a lead submits via ad form: GHL captures contact > triggers Aria outbound call within 60 seconds > Aria qualifies and books. No human required.
Us
✓
Google Ads setup (Pro + Partner only)
Search campaigns for high-intent keywords ("[treatment] near me", "[clinic type] [suburb]"). Landing page connected to GHL for lead capture.
Us
✓
GEO & local search optimisation (Pro + Partner only)
Google Business Profile audit. Keyword-optimised description, services, Q&A. Review response templates. Local citation check.
Us
04
Automation & Nurture
Day 4-6
✓
Website chat widget installed
Embed Aria chat widget on clinic's website. Matches their brand colours. Handles FAQs, service questions, and routes to booking.
Us
✓
DM automation configured
Instagram and Facebook DM auto-replies. Keyword triggers for common questions. Routes booking requests to Aria or direct calendar link.
Us
✓
SMS & email nurture sequences built
Post-enquiry: 3-touch follow-up sequence (immediate, 24hr, 72hr). Post-booking: confirmation + reminder + prep instructions. No-show: re-engagement sequence.
Us
✓
Missed call recovery
If Aria can't reach a lead by phone, automatic SMS with booking link is sent. "Hi [name], we just tried to call you about your enquiry. Book a time that suits you: [link]"
Us
05
Go Live & Verify
Day 7
✓
Full end-to-end test
Run the complete flow: ad impression > lead form > Aria calls in under 60 seconds > books appointment > confirmation email + SMS sent > shows in GHL calendar. Test on mobile and desktop.
Us
✓
Client walkthrough call
Show the client their live dashboard. Walk through how leads appear, how Aria handles calls, where bookings show up. Answer questions. Confirm they know where to check performance.
Us Client
✓
Ads go live
Launch campaigns. Monitor first 24 hours closely. Verify lead flow, Aria call quality, and booking accuracy. Adjust targeting or creative if needed.
Us
Go-live criteria: All 5 phases complete. Minimum 5 test calls passed. Aria booking into correct calendar. Confirmation emails/SMS sending. Ads approved by Meta. Client has seen their dashboard.
