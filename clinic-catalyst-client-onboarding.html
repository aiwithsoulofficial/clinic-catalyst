04
Common Questions
Do I need to manage the ads myself?

No. We build, launch, and manage everything. You'll see the campaigns for approval, but we handle all the day-to-day optimisation.

Will patients know they're talking to AI?

Aria is designed to be conversational and natural. She knows your clinic, your services, and your team by name. Most patients don't notice - and more importantly, they book. The alternative is a missed call and a lost patient.

What about ad spend?

Ad spend is separate from your monthly package. You control the budget directly - there are no minimums or maximums. We'll recommend a starting budget based on your area and goals during the onboarding call.

What if I need to change my services or pricing?

Just let us know. We update Aria's knowledge, your ad creative, and your automations to reflect any changes. That's part of the ongoing management.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Month-to-month, 30 days notice. No exit fees, no penalties. Your CRM data is yours.
